Ken's Supply Co

Role: Systems design, operations
Tools: Airtable, Sheets, Logistics planning
Timeline: 3 months

Context

A small maker collective needed a reliable system for sourcing, managing, and distributing supplies to members. Manual processes led to bottlenecks, inventory mismatches, and inefficient communication.

Strategy

  • Map existing workflows to identify pain points
  • Design centralized database for inventory tracking
  • Automate ordering and distribution notifications
  • Create role-based access for different team members

Execution

  • Built inventory management system in Airtable with custom workflows
  • Set up automated alerts for low stock and pending orders
  • Created member-facing portal for requesting and tracking supplies
  • Documented procedures for onboarding new suppliers and members
  • Trained team on system usage and maintenance

Outcome

  • Reduced order fulfillment time by 60%
  • Eliminated stockouts through proactive tracking
  • System in use and scaled to support 2x membership growth
