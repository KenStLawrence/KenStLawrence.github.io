Ponciano Seoane EPK

Role: Creative direction, design
Tools: Framer, Figma
Timeline: 6 weeks

Context

Ponciano Seoane needed a modern press kit and portfolio presence. Traditional EPK formats (PDF, email-based) were static and didn't showcase the full breadth of work or invite further exploration.

Strategy

  • Design for discovery: organize work thematically to highlight range
  • Reduce friction: embed media inline, make everything accessible from one URL
  • Reflect brand: clean, intentional design matching artistic aesthetic
  • Mobile-first: ensure journalists and curators could browse on any device

Execution

  • Built in Framer for rapid iteration and responsive design
  • Organized portfolio into thematic sections (performances, installations, media)
  • Embedded video and high-res image galleries
  • Added press contact and bio sections for easy journalist outreach
  • Optimized for fast loading and smooth interactions

Outcome

  • Deployed as live site, shared widely with press and curators
  • Simplified communication of complex artistic practice
  • Served as template for future digital press kits
