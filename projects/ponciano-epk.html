EPK Maker

Role: Strategy, copy architecture, implementation
Tools: Framer (free tier)
Timeline: ~2 days to live prototype
Status: Live, refinement phase

Context

An independent recording artist with televised exposure and strong live experience lacked a unified, booking-forward press asset. Existing presence was fragmented across social and streaming platforms, with no structured authority narrative or professional “sendable” link for buyers.

Objective

  • Consolidate credibility into a structured authority narrative
  • Reduce friction for wedding, corporate, and venue buyers
  • Provide a modular asset for iterative upgrades

Strategy

1. Authority Structuring

  • Frame televised exposure and public praise as buyer-legible credibility
  • Position competitive context as caliber rather than weakness

2. Booking-Forward Hierarchy

  • Clear positioning
  • Public validation signals
  • Performance formats
  • Testimonials
  • Embedded media
  • Direct booking call-to-action

Biography was treated as secondary to clarity and conversion.

3. Deployment-First Execution

  • Deployed within ~48 hours
  • Enabled live stakeholder feedback
  • Planned iterative refinement post-deployment

4. Human–AI Workflow

  • Early phase: AI used for standards research and structural blueprinting
  • Mid phase: manual layout control increased with platform proficiency
  • Final phase: AI used selectively for copy tightening and debugging

AI functioned as an accelerator, not a substitute for judgment.

Execution

  • Built in Framer under real platform constraints
  • Resolved layout instability from fixed-height containers
  • Corrected overflow clipping issues
  • Worked around embed component limitations
  • Rebuilt unstable wrapper frames using modular, fit-content layout structure

Outcome

  • Live deployed asset within ~2 days
  • Positive client engagement and collaborative refinement plan
  • Real client delivery under time and tool constraints

What This Demonstrates

  • Rapid domain and tool onboarding
  • Authority stacking without exaggeration
  • Conversion-oriented design thinking
  • Debugging under platform friction
  • AI-assisted workflow discipline
