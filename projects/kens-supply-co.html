AI-Assisted eCommerce Workflow

Role: Workflow design, AI operations, listing execution
Tools: LLM-assisted prompting, eBay platform tools, USPS logistics
Timeline: 3 months
Results: ~$2,000 revenue | 30 transactions | 100% positive feedback

Context

Manual listing of collectible inventory (retro games, consoles, comics, knives) was repetitive and time-intensive. Each listing required title writing, price research, categorization, shipping estimation, and sales strategy decisions. Average listing time ranged from 15–25 minutes per item.

The objective was to design a repeatable, AI-assisted workflow that reduced listing time without sacrificing pricing accuracy, authenticity verification, or buyer trust.

Strategy

  • Separate repetitive formatting tasks from human judgment
  • Harden output structure to eliminate drift
  • Standardize shipping method (USPS Ground Advantage)
  • Maintain human control over pricing validation and risk decisions

Execution

Phase 1 – Exploratory Iteration

  • Tested flexible prompts in live operational conditions
  • Identified failure modes: format inconsistency, verbosity, pricing drift, context bleed
  • Observed that variability increased correction time

Phase 2 – Constraint Hardening

  • Introduced strict output template:
    • Selling strategy summary
    • Copy-paste title
    • Categorization information
    • Copy-paste description
    • Shipping weight and dimensions
    • Pricing recommendation (BIN vs. auction with rationale)
  • Eliminated structural drift
  • Treated model as formatting and research accelerator, not decision authority

Phase 3 – Operational Scaling

  • ~5 minutes: photograph item
  • ~1 minute: upload and verify authenticity
  • ~2 minutes: validate pricing
  • ~1 minute: finalize listing
  • Average total time: ~5–7 minutes per listing
  • Standardized shipping reduced post-sale friction
  • Inventory pacing maintained consistent account activity

Workflow Design

AI Responsibilities

  • Structured listing generation
  • Comparable price approximation
  • Shipping estimate standardization
  • Tone normalization

Human Responsibilities

  • Authenticity verification
  • Condition interpretation
  • Pricing validation
  • Account pacing strategy
  • Risk management

Outcome

  • ~$2,000 revenue generated
  • 30 completed transactions
  • 100% positive feedback
  • Listing time reduced from ~20 minutes to 5–7 minutes
  • Standardized output across varied inventory categories
